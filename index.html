About Me

With extensive experience in geospatial analysis, I bring together technical expertise and strategic thinking to help clients navigate challenging landscapes. My work is focused on two core areas:

  • GIS in Politics: I have collaborated on political campaigns, designed initiatives, and provided comprehensive election analyses. From mapping voter demographics to optimizing resource allocation, my GIS solutions empower political decision-making.
  • Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching: I apply advanced remote sensing techniques to monitor crop health, assess land use, and support sustainable practices. My insights help clients improve yield, manage resources, and reduce environmental risks.
