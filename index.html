About Me

With extensive experience in geospatial analysis, I blend technical expertise and strategic thinking to help clients navigate complex challenges. My work is focused on two core areas:

  • Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching: Leveraging satellite imagery, drone data, and advanced analytics to monitor crop health, assess water resources, and estimate soil moisture.
  • GIS in Politics: I apply spatial analysis to political campaigns, initiatives, and elections using R for my analyses. I’m also experienced with ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, QGIS, and Carto, which allows me to tailor solutions to meet diverse project needs.
