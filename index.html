About Me

With extensive experience in geospatial analysis, I blend technical expertise and strategic thinking to help clients navigate complex challenges. My work is focused on two core areas:

  • Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching: Leveraging satellite imagery, drone data, and advanced analytics to monitor crop health, assess water resources, estimate soil moisture, and more.
  • GIS in Politics: Applying spatial analysis to political campaigns, initiatives, and elections to provide insights into voter behavior, districting, and engagement.

Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching

Crop Health Monitoring with NDVI

Utilize satellite imagery (Sentinel-2 or Landsat) to calculate NDVI and assess crop health over time.

Drought Assessment & Water Resource Management

Analyze precipitation data and indices like SPI and NDWI to detect drought trends affecting farms.

Soil Moisture Estimation for Irrigation

Leverage Sentinel-1 SAR data and machine learning in R to predict soil moisture conditions for optimal irrigation.

Livestock Grazing Land Assessment

Analyze time-series vegetation indices to identify overgrazed or underutilized pasture lands.

Wildfire Risk Assessment

Utilize MODIS/VIIRS fire data and weather variables to model wildfire risk in agricultural areas.

Precision Agriculture with UAV Data

Process high-resolution drone imagery in R to create prescription maps for targeted fertilization and irrigation.
