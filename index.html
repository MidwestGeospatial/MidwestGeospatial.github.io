About

I'm a geospatial professional with deep experience turning complex location data into products that support real decisions—whether that's a planning department weighing infrastructure investments, a public safety agency allocating patrol resources, or an organization trying to understand who and what is at risk.

My work spans spatial analysis and risk modeling, interactive web mapping, and reproducible data workflows. I build outputs designed to be used: dashboards and maps built for briefings and stakeholder discussions, analysis pipelines that can be updated as new data arrives, and deliverables that translate spatial complexity into language decision-makers can act on.

  • Tools: ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, QGIS, R, Carto
  • Strengths: Hotspot and risk analysis, flood and environmental mapping, housing and equity analysis, traffic safety, interactive web maps, reproducible R workflows
  • Clients served: Municipalities, public agencies, planning organizations, and private organizations

Services

Spatial Analysis & Risk Mapping

Hotspot detection, risk surface modeling, and cluster analysis for public safety, infrastructure, environmental, and equity applications. Outputs are designed for briefings, grant applications, and operational planning.

Interactive Web Maps & Dashboards

Leaflet-based web maps hosted on GitHub Pages or ArcGIS Online—filterable, layered, and built for public-facing or internal stakeholder use. No GIS software required to view or share.

Flood & Environmental Risk

Flood exposure mapping paired with population and infrastructure context. Parcel-level refinement available where data permits—useful for capital improvement planning, insurance analysis, and hazard mitigation grants.

Reproducible GIS Workflows

Analysis built in R with documented, repeatable scripts. Workflows can be handed off, updated with new data, or adapted to new geographies without starting from scratch.

Reporting & Briefing Support

Static maps, exported figures, and summary outputs formatted for reports, presentations, and stakeholder briefings—ready to drop into Word, PowerPoint, or PDF deliverables.

Political & Campaign GIS

Precinct-level spatial models, turnout and persuasion mapping, canvassing optimization, and cluster detection for campaigns and political organizations. Portfolio samples coming soon.

Interactive Web Maps

A curated set of applied GIS projects, each built around a real decision-making use case. The underlying methodology adapts to any jurisdiction—reach out if you'd like to discuss applying a similar approach to your data. Each map opens in a new tab.
