About Me

With extensive experience in geospatial analysis, I blend technical expertise and strategic thinking to help clients navigate complex challenges. My work is focused on two core areas:

  • Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching: Leveraging satellite imagery, drone data, and advanced analytics to monitor crop health, assess water resources, and estimate soil moisture.
  • GIS in Politics: Applying spatial analysis to political campaigns, initiatives, and elections using R packages such as **sf**, **leaflet**, **tmap**, and **ggplot2**.

Remote Sensing for Agriculture & Ranching

Crop Health Monitoring with NDVI

Utilize satellite imagery (Sentinel-2 or Landsat) to calculate NDVI and assess crop health over time.

Drought Assessment & Water Resource Management

Analyze precipitation data and indices like SPI and NDWI to detect drought trends affecting farms.

Soil Moisture Estimation for Irrigation

Leverage Sentinel-1 SAR data and machine learning in R to predict soil moisture for optimal irrigation.

Livestock Grazing Land Assessment

Analyze time-series vegetation indices to identify overgrazed or underutilized pasture lands.

Wildfire Risk Assessment

Utilize MODIS/VIIRS fire data and weather variables to model wildfire risk in agricultural areas.

Precision Agriculture with UAV Data

Process high-resolution drone imagery in R to create prescription maps for targeted fertilization and irrigation.

GIS in Politics

Campaign Strategy Map

Use voter data, demographics, and past election results with **sf** and **leaflet** to pinpoint swing regions and target campaign outreach.

Election Results Visualization

Create detailed choropleth maps of historical election outcomes using **ggplot2** combined with **sf** for a publication-ready look.

Voter Demographics Heatmap

Build a static or interactive heatmap showcasing voter demographics (age, income, education) across regions using **tmap** and **sf**.

Political Boundary Redistricting Analysis

Analyze current political boundaries and explore alternative districting scenarios using spatial operations in **sf** with visualizations in **tmap** or **leaflet**.

Constituency Participation Mapping

Map the spatial distribution of campaign events, volunteer networks, and voter turnout using **leaflet** and complement with **ggplot2** visualizations.

Spatial Analysis of Political Polling Data

Combine polling data with geographic information to reveal spatial patterns in voter sentiment and turnout using **sf**, **tmap/leaflet**, and **ggplot2**.
